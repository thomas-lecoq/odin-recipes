Burger recipe

Description

A juicy beef patty seared to perfection, layered with melted cheddar, crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, and caramelized onions. Nestled in a toasted brioche bun, it’s pure flavor harmony.

Tangy pickles, smoky bacon, and a dollop of garlic aioli elevate every bite. The buttery bun soaks the juices just right. This burger recipe defines indulgence and perfect balance.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty (80/20)
  • Sea salt & black pepper
  • Cheddar slices
  • Brioche buns
  • Unsalted butter (for toasting)
  • Crisp lettuce
  • Ripe tomato slices
  • Red onion (thinly sliced)
  • Pickle slices
  • Smoked bacon
  • Garlic aioli
  • Ketchup and mustard

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and set up mise en place: patty, cheese, buns, vegetables, bacon, aioli, condiments.
  2. Slice tomato, onion; wash and dry lettuce; warm pickles; preheat skillet or grill.
  3. Form 180–200g patties, press slight dimple in center; season both sides with salt and pepper.
  4. Cook bacon until crisp; keep warm on paper towel.
  5. Sear sliced onions in pan with a little butter until caramelized; remove and reserve.
  6. Heat skillet to high, add patties; sear 3–4 minutes per side for medium (adjust to preference).
  7. Place cheddar on patties in final minute; cover to melt.
  8. Butter and toast brioche buns in pan until golden.
  9. Spread garlic aioli on bottom bun, add lettuce and tomato, then patty with melted cheese.
  10. Add caramelized onions, pickles, bacon, a smear of ketchup and mustard to taste.
  11. Cap with top bun, press briefly to set, rest 1 minute, then serve immediately.
