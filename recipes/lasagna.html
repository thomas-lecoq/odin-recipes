Lasagna recipe

Description

Layers of tender pasta sheets, rich beef ragù, creamy béchamel, and melted mozzarella create deep, comforting flavor. Each bite balances savory richness with a delicate, satisfying texture.

Slow-simmered sauce enhances the warmth of herbs and garlic. Golden crusted cheese seals perfection. This lasagna recipe defines Italian comfort and culinary elegance in every serving.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets (dried or fresh)
  • Olive oil
  • Onion, finely chopped
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Ground beef (or mix beef/pork)
  • Crushed tomatoes or passata
  • Tomato paste
  • Red wine (optional)
  • Italian seasoning (oregano, basil)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Unsalted butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk (whole)
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until translucent.
  3. Brown ground meat; drain excess fat.
  4. Add tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, wine, seasoning; simmer 20–40 minutes. Adjust salt.
  5. Prepare béchamel: melt butter, whisk in flour, gradually add milk until smooth and thick; season.
  6. Boil or blister lasagna sheets if required; drain and cool.
  7. Layer: sauce, pasta, béchamel, Parmesan, mozzarella; repeat to top.
  8. Finish with generous mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  9. Bake 30–40 minutes until bubbling and golden.
  10. Rest 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
